Who am I



Hello everyone, I am Alexandru Clapa. For those of you who haven't met me yet, I'm a young software developer currently living in Bucharest, Romania. For the past several years I've focused on mobile development, specifically iOS, Android and Windows Phone. I have some experience in creating desktop class apps for Windows and OS X. I am currently learning front and back end dev. I enjoy making people's lives easier and saving their time by making software that fill these gaps.

I am capable of managing work on my own. I used to work all by myself and constantly trying to learn new things. I love spending my time discovering new things, watching F1, going for a run or laughing with my friends.

I'll be happy to meet new people to talk about new technologies or future projects and startups. You can always say hello or talk with me about future collaborations by sending me an email at me@alexandruclapa.com.
